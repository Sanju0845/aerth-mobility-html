Founder Story

Building India's EV Service Backbone with Purpose and Vision

Discover the philosophy, vision, and mission behind Aerth Mobility — a journey to create the service ecosystem that powers India's electric mobility revolution.

Why the Name "AERTH"?

The name AERTH represents the core philosophy behind the company.

It combines the spirit of Earth — our shared responsibility to build a cleaner and more sustainable planet — with the future of mobility powered by electric technology.

Electric mobility is not just about vehicles. It is about protecting the environment, reducing pollution, and building smarter transportation systems for the next generations.

AERTH Mobility was born from this belief: that sustainable transportation requires not only innovative vehicles but also a reliable ecosystem that supports them throughout their lifecycle.

The name reflects a commitment to technology, sustainability, and trust.

Every service centre, every trained technician, and every repaired electric vehicle represents one step toward a cleaner and more efficient mobility future.

AERTH Mobility stands for mobility that respects the Earth while powering the future.

Founder's Message

When electric mobility began gaining momentum in India, I noticed something important.

While companies were focusing on manufacturing electric vehicles, very few were building the service infrastructure needed to support them at scale.

Without a strong service ecosystem, even the best vehicles cannot deliver a reliable ownership experience.

That realization became the foundation of AERTH Mobility.

Our vision is simple but ambitious: to build a trusted nationwide service network dedicated to electric two-wheelers and three-wheelers.

We want EV owners, fleet operators, and manufacturers to know that wherever their vehicles travel, professional service support will be available.

“

AERTH Mobility is not just a company. It is a mission to create the service backbone of India's electric mobility revolution.

— Founder, AERTH Mobility

Vision 2030

By 2030, AERTH Mobility aims to become India's most trusted EV service ecosystem.

Our long-term vision includes:

A PAN-India network of EV service centres

Thousands of trained EV technicians

Strategic partnerships with EV manufacturers and battery companies

Advanced diagnostic and repair technologies

A nationwide platform supporting millions of electric vehicles

As electric mobility scales across India, AERTH Mobility intends to become the after-sales infrastructure powering this transformation.

The AERTH Mobility Manifesto

We believe electric mobility will reshape the future of transportation.

But vehicles alone cannot build a revolution.

Every electric vehicle needs reliable service, skilled technicians, and a dependable ecosystem behind it.

At AERTH Mobility, we believe:

•

EV owners deserve reliable and transparent service

•

Technicians deserve specialized training and opportunities

•

Manufacturers need trusted service partners

•

Electric mobility must be supported by strong infrastructure

We are building a future where electric vehicle ownership is simple, reliable, and worry-free.

“

Our mission is to power the service ecosystem that electric mobility depends on.

— AERTH Mobility
