AERTH EV Skill Center

Building India's Next Generation of Electric Vehicle Technicians

The Future of Electric Mobility Needs Skilled Technicians

As electric vehicles rapidly grow across India, the demand for trained EV technicians is increasing dramatically.

AERTH Mobility established the EV Skill Center to develop specialized technical talent capable of servicing electric two-wheelers and three-wheelers.

Through structured training, practical workshops, and industry exposure, the AERTH Skill Center prepares technicians to support the rapidly expanding electric mobility service ecosystem.

The Growing Demand for EV Technicians

India is expected to have millions of electric two-wheelers and three-wheelers on the road in the coming years. Maintaining and servicing these vehicles requires specialized technical knowledge that traditional automotive training does not cover.

The AERTH Skill Center focuses on hands-on EV training designed specifically for electric mobility systems including batteries, motors, controllers, and charging systems.
